Nehru Planetarium

24 Aug 2016

Nehru Planetarium is a prominent science and space centre, which attracts plenty of curious space aspiring students. It is immensely popular among scientists and scholars meeting for discussions and lectures, on various stellar and Astronomical events. Established in 1977 and inaugurated by the then Prime Minister, Indira Gandhi, the palnetarium is a dome-shaped building that houses an art gallery, restaurant, library and a cultural center. Astronomy club, IIT Bombay conducts a visit to the planetarium every year in autumn semester.
